# How to Request an Itemized Medical Bill

Receiving a massive hospital bill can be a terrifying experience. But before you panic, empty your savings, or reach for your credit card, there is one crucial step every American patient must take: **always ask for an itemized medical bill**.

### Why is this so important?
When U.S. hospitals send you a standard summary bill, it usually just shows a massive total amount or vague categories. Studies suggest that up to 80% of U.S. medical bills contain errors. You might be charged for:

* Medications you never received
* Duplicate procedures
* Canceled tests

Without an itemized statement, these hidden overcharges remain completely invisible. Under federal guidelines, you have the absolute legal right to request this detailed breakdown from your provider.

### What to do next?
Once you have your itemized bill in hand, the real work begins. Find the 5-digit CPT codes and use [MedFair's free tool](https://medfair.us) to compare your hospital’s inflated charges against objective CMS Medicare reference prices.
